# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small-to-medium UK businesses actively looking for a digital agency: founders, marketing managers, and operators who want tangible outcomes (more customers, stronger brand, faster growth) rather than agency theatre. They are busy, skeptical of fluff, and need to trust quickly. They land on the site to evaluate whether Vazgro is credible and worth a conversation.

## Product Purpose

Vazgro is a London-based UK digital agency offering three service lines: LAUNCH (fixed-fee packages from £149), GROW (monthly subscriptions from £349/mo), and BUILD (custom apps and AI from £3,999). The site exists to convert qualified visitors into booked discovery calls by projecting credibility, directness, and premium craft. Success is a visitor booking a call or sending an email within one session.

## Brand Personality

Premium, credible, direct.

## Anti-references

Generic Wix/Squarespace agency templates. Over-animated VC-startup aesthetics. Design-award-chasing studios that bury the offer. Cluttered multi-column agency sites where the portfolio comes before the value proposition.

## Design Principles

1. **Credibility before decoration.** Every element should make a skeptical business owner more confident, not more dazzled. Restraint is a premium signal.
2. **Outcome-first copy.** Lead with what the client gets, not what Vazgro does. Services framed around results.
3. **Hierarchy is non-negotiable.** The most important thing on any page must be obvious within two seconds. Space and weight do more than color.
4. **Same design pattern, raised execution.** The existing visual language (white, yellow accent, uppercase Manrope tracking, grid-tight layouts) is right. The opportunity is in spacing rhythm, density variation, and structural clarity.
5. **Direct paths.** Every section should have one clear next action. No dead ends, no buried CTAs.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Sufficient contrast on all text. No motion-only affordances.
